YtStream

Last updated: June 25, 2026

YtStream ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Android application YtStream handles your information.

🔒 YtStream does NOT collect, store, or share any personal data. All processing happens locally on your device.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone, etc.)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or crash reports sent to our servers
  • Audio or video content from your streams
  • YouTube stream keys or account credentials

2. Permissions Used and Why

  • Screen Capture (MediaProjection) — Required to capture your device screen for live streaming. Screen content is streamed directly to YouTube via RTMP and is never stored or transmitted to us.
  • Microphone (RECORD_AUDIO) — Used only when you select "Mic + Internal" audio mode to capture your voice for live commentary. Audio is sent directly to YouTube and not stored by us.
  • Internet (INTERNET) — Required to transmit the live stream to YouTube's RTMP servers.
  • Foreground Service — Required to keep the stream running while the app is in the background.
  • Display Over Other Apps (SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW) — Used to show floating Pause/Mute/Stop controls while you use other apps during streaming.
  • Wake Lock (WAKE_LOCK) — Prevents the device CPU from sleeping so the stream continues uninterrupted.

3. YouTube / Google Services

YtStream streams video directly to YouTube using the YouTube Live RTMP protocol. Your stream key is stored locally on your device using Android's SharedPreferences and is never sent to our servers. Your use of YouTube is governed by YouTube's Terms of Service and Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Data Storage

The only data stored by YtStream is:

  • Your YouTube Stream Key — stored locally on your device only, in Android SharedPreferences
  • Your preferences (audio mode, orientation, theme) — stored locally on your device only

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

5. Third-Party Libraries

YtStream uses the following open-source libraries:

  • RootEncoder (Pedro S. G.) — For RTMP streaming and screen capture. This library operates entirely on-device and does not transmit data to third parties.
  • Flutter (Google) — UI framework. No data is sent to Google by Flutter itself.

6. Children's Privacy

YtStream is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no data to access, correct, or delete. If you have concerns, please contact us.
